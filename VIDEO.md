# Plan AI - Video Promo Script

**Target Audience:** Engineering Managers, Product Managers, Tech Leads, Software Agency Owners.
**Core Message:** The AI meeting assistant that actually understands code and ships deliverables, not just summaries.
**Estimated Length:** 60-90 seconds
**Vibe:** Fast-paced, technical but accessible, solving a real pain point.

---

## Scene 1: The Pain (0:00 - 0:10)
**Visual:** 
Fast-paced montage. A blurry Zoom gallery view. A developer looking stressed. A Jira board with empty or poorly written tickets. A Slack message: *"Did anyone write down what we agreed on for the auth refactor?"*
**Audio (SFX):** 
Keyboard typing, notifications pinging, a sigh.
**Voiceover:** 
"You just spent an hour in a technical sync. Great decisions were made. But now... someone has to translate that into Jira tickets, update the specs, and draw the architecture diagram."

## Scene 2: The Hook & Introduction (0:10 - 0:20)
**Visual:** 
Screen wipe. Bold text appears: **Enter Plan AI**.
Cut to the Plan AI Desktop Recorder sitting sleekly on a macOS desktop, clicking "Record".
**Voiceover:** 
"Meet Plan AI. It's not just another meeting transcriber. It's the AI workspace purpose-built for software teams."

## Scene 3: How it Works - Code Context (0:20 - 0:35)
**Visual:** 
Split screen. On the left, a meeting transcript discussing the `verifyToken` function. On the right, GitNexus analyzing the code graph. The AI automatically links the conversation to the actual repository code.
**Voiceover:** 
"Generic AI tools don't understand your codebase. Plan AI does. With native GitNexus integration, it knows your architecture, your functions, and your repos. When you talk about the auth service, it actually knows what that means."

## Scene 4: The Deliverables (0:35 - 0:55)
**Visual:** 
Fast, punchy cuts showing the automated outputs:
1. An action item turning into a perfectly scoped Linear ticket with acceptance criteria.
2. A Mermaid.js architecture diagram generating itself in real-time.
3. A technical spec document being formatted.
4. Branded PowerPoint slides summarizing the Q2 roadmap discussion.
**Voiceover:** 
"From conversation to deliverable in minutes. Plan AI doesn't just give you a summary—it automatically ships properly scoped Jira or Linear tickets, writes technical markdown docs, and even generates architecture diagrams and branded slides. Instantly."

## Scene 5: Multi-platform & BYOK (0:55 - 1:10)
**Visual:** 
Quick shots of the Mobile Companion app recording an in-person whiteboard session. 
Cut to the billing/settings screen showing "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK).
**Voiceover:** 
"Record virtual calls natively on Mac, or use the mobile app for in-person whiteboard sessions. And the best part? No per-seat AI markup. Bring your own OpenRouter and Deepgram keys, and pay only for what you use."

## Scene 6: Outro & Call to Action (1:10 - 1:20)
**Visual:** 
Plan AI logo. Text: "From conversation to deliverable in minutes, not days."
URL on screen: `plan-ai.blueberrybytes.com`
**Voiceover:** 
"Stop writing tickets from memory. Start shipping. Try Plan AI today."

---

## 🎬 Production Notes for the Creator
* **B-Roll:** Ensure UI recordings highlight the sleek dark mode (or user's preferred theme).
* **Pacing:** Keep the transitions snappy when showing the generated artifacts (tickets -> docs -> diagrams -> slides). This is the "WOW" moment.
* **Music:** Upbeat, modern electronic/lo-fi track to keep the momentum going.
